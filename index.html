Ingredient Now

Ingredient Now gives you access to hundreds of yummy recipes from a simple keyword and will generate a shopping list from the recipe. To get started type a keyword into the search bar such as chicken, tacos, or korean, and we will recommend some recipes. If you see one you like, simply press "Get", and we will generate the recipe list.
